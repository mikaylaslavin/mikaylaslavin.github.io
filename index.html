about me

I'm a UX designer aiming to amplify the human experience through accessible and meaningful designs. I'm currently designing @ the MSU Burgess Institute for Entrepreneurship and Innovation!

I recently graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Information Science from Michigan State University. I concentrated on Human Centered Technologies and pursued minors in Graphic Design and Business. Furthermore, I was heavily involved in designing for student startup ventures at the MSU Burgess Institute for Entrepreneurship and Innovation. Alongside, I served as the Vice President of the Michigan State Chapter of the American Institute of Graphic Arts and was a member of the MSU Student Allocation Board. Now that I've graduated, I'm looking forward to furthering my education with a Masters in HCI, set to graduate in May 2025. My ultimate goal is to pursue a career in UX Design. This summer, I'm excited to embark on a new journey as a Marketing Design Intern at Lenovo.
